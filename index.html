About Us

At Neon Brown Studios, we're passionate about creating atmospheric horror experiences that challenge conventions and push boundaries. Our small but dedicated team combines innovative gameplay mechanics with haunting narratives to create unforgettable gaming experiences.

Founded in the heart of the indie game development scene, we believe in crafting games that not only terrify but also tell meaningful stories. Our approach combines classic horror elements with modern game design, creating experiences that stick with players long after they've finished playing.

Projects

Subject 19

Dive into a psychological horror experience where memories and reality blur. As Subject 19, navigate through a mysterious research facility where each corridor holds secrets of your past. Uncover the truth behind the experiments while avoiding the horrors that now roam these halls.

Features:

  • • Dynamic sanity system that affects gameplay and environment
  • • Intricate puzzle-solving mechanics
  • • Multiple endings based on your choices
  • • Original soundtrack inspired by 70s horror films
