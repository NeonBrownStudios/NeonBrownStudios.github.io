About Me

I'm Devendr, an independent game developer and founder of Neon Brown Studios. My passion lies in immersive, story-driven experiences with psychological and surreal twists. I was honored with a BAFTA award for excellence in narrative design.

With years of experience crafting unique game worlds, I focus on blending narrative depth with gameplay innovation to create unforgettable player journeys.

My Game

I’m currently developing Subject 19, a narrative survival horror game exploring distorted realities and the human psyche. It’s designed to be replayable, with shifting choices and mind-bending environments.

The game challenges players to navigate a constantly shifting mental hospital, uncover hidden lore, and face psychological horrors both real and imagined.
